Our School

At Ibadur Rahman Academy, we understand that a holistic education goes beyond preparing students for exams and future careers. We strive to empower our students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to lead fulfilling lives, make positive contributions to society, and navigate the complexities of the modern world. By nurturing their intellectual, emotional, physical, and social development, we aim to shape well-rounded individuals who are ready to embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

Beyond the classroom, our school offers a wide range of co-curricular activities and community service opportunities. Students have the chance to explore their interests, discover new talents, and develop leadership skills through clubs, societies, and volunteer programs. These experiences enhance their personal growth, foster a sense of civic responsibility, and encourage a lifelong love of learning.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMMES

The school offers a three year Senior High School course. The curriculum of the school consists of core subjects namely: English Language, Integrated science, Mathematics, Social Studies, ICT, and PE and four specialized programmes which are General Science, General Arts, Business and Visual Arts..
